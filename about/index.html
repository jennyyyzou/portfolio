Hi I'm Jenny. I enjoy exploring the design field of user experience through iterative process and inspiration I find in my daily life. I have grown to comprehend that design is all about a way of communicating and thinking, particularly being able to understand people and help people understand.

My desire is to use storytelling, graphic elements and iteration to problem-solving for developing human-oriented projects that builds communities. If you'd like to chat over tea or a beer about collaborations, opportunities, or food, shoot me an email!
